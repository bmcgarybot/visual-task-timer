Before You Begin
What This App Does
EMI-Tech guides your child's eyes through smooth, calming movement patterns based on Eye Movement Integration therapy. The movements help the brain de-escalate during moments of distress. Breathing exercises between patterns promote regulation.
How Vibration Works
The full EMI-Tech device uses vibrating handles that pulse in sync with the visual patterns. As the orb moves left, the left handle vibrates stronger. As it moves right, the right handle takes over.
Using a Game Controller
Connect a Bluetooth game controller (Xbox, PlayStation, Joy-Con) to feel the bilateral vibration. The controller's left and right rumble motors will pulse with the orb movement.
No controller detected
Use Headphones
A calming tone pans from left ear to right ear following the orb. With headphones, this creates an auditory version of the bilateral stimulation.
